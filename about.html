About Us

Take It To The Streets is a nonprofit organization aimed to help motorists contest and win appeals on parking ticket violations in the City of Chicago. Our goal is to education people on how to appeal and win in court.

Who does this apply to?

Requirements:

  • Lives within the City of Chicago
  • Has recieved a ticket within the past 7 days
  • Registered owner of the ticketed vehicle

More information in the Info Tab

Why does this matter?

Often times, motorists default their actions on paying the violation ticket rather than disputing it in court. However, some motorists cannot afford to pay the ticket because they have other fees to pay like the annual city sticker that would cost around $50-$200 depending on the vehicle.
